What We Do

Growth Story helps parents organize their child’s achievements — so milestones don’t get lost in endless photo libraries.

We organize memories which you can privately share and keep everything in one place.

In future, you’ll even be able to create keepsake books or pull highlights for college applications.

Who Is This For

Growth Story is built for intentional parents of kids aged 8–16 who want to capture what matters — proud moments, joyful memories, and milestones along the way.

Whether you’re the quietly ambitious parent, proactively supporting your child’s growth and future opportunities, or the memory keeper, wanting to preserve their childhood story before it slips away — Growth Story helps you stay organized and proud of the journey you're on together.

What's In It For Me?

Memories, Neatly Organized: No more endless scrolling — easily save and find your child’s proudest moments.

Timely Nudges, Not More Work: Get gentle reminders regularly or after specific events so you never miss capturing a milestone.

A Living Record of Growth: Build a beautiful portfolio to reflect on how far your child has come — or even use for college and scholarship applications.
